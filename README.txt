Stand out from the crowd in Google search results and increase your traffic and conversions by adding your reviews to your Google listings!

== Description ==

As you will probably know Google is a crowded place serving up search results that Google feels are relevant your query.
With hReview for WordPress you can stand out from the crowd in Google search results by making your product or service review show in listings with review stars, price ranges and a reviewer.

[Support](http://www.robertmkelly.co.uk/category/plugins/hreview-for-wordpress/) |
[Change Log](http://www.robertmkelly.co.uk/category/plugins/hreview-for-wordpress/) |
[FAQ](http://www.robertmkelly.co.uk/category/plugins/hreview-for-wordpress/)

Features included:

* Add stars to your Google listing
* Add pricing information to your Google listing (all currencies supported)
* Add details of a reviewer to your Google listing
* If you have your Google+ authorship verified you can show your image next to your review in Google
* The pottential to increase traffic and conversions!

**If upgrading, please back up your database first!**

== Installation ==

1. Upload `rk-wp-hreview` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Create or edit a page or post and enter your rating and other details into the box shown

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do get support? =

Email all support questions to wp@robertmkelly.co.uk

= My Google result is not showing my review =

Unfortunately adding hReview markup does not guarantee that your review will show in Google. Google have complete control of weather your review will show in the stars format or not through their complex algorithm.

= How do i add Google authorship to my page?  =

It's really easy to setup and you need a Google+ account to do so. [Click here for more information on Google authorship](https://plus.google.com/authorship)

= How can i see what will show in Google?  =

You can preview what your review may look like in Google using the rich snippet testing tool, [Click here for more information](http://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/richsnippets)
